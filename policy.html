Label Policy






Frequently Asked Question

Our label policy is there in general so that you know how we process your demo/tracks, how we handle distribution, what's your right as an artist or individual, revenue sharing, copyright, and other important aspects. The policy is there so that we can remain transparent, fair, and have a safe environment for all artists.


Yobimo and the artist itself both holds the ownership of the track. But, it's important to note that artists still retain the full ownership of their track, and Yobimo will be the one licensing and distribute the track on their behalf. Overall, the primary rights holder is the artist


To protect everyone's intellectual property, it is essential that track/material you submit is original or you have full permission to use it. This is important to ensure we avoid any copyright disputes, as well as to protect all artists' work.


Materials published under Yobimo may be used only for the sole of promotion. Yobimo will not alter, remix, or use the track outside of Yobimo's activities without the artist's explicit permission.


If a copyright dispute arises, we will notify the artist and if necessary, we will remove the material from distribution until it's resolved. However, please take note that you're responsible for resolving any disputes related to your track/material


Sure! If you have any questions or concerns about the policy, you may reach out to staff.yobimo@gmail.com and we're happy to clarify any details.
